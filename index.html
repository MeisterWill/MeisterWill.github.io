Project Synopsis

WexEnergy is a company that is geared towards sustainability and reducing carbon emissions with unique window insulation technology. Their products, PolarSkinTM and SolarSkinTM are window insulation panels, designed to decrease energy flow through existing windows for both commercial and residential buildings. These products help to maintain interior heat in the winter time and keep it out in the summer helping customersâ€™ comfort while also combining window functionality and sustainability. Our job as a team is to create an intuitive mobile application where customers and distributors (both residential and enterprise) can easily use the app to measure windows, capture their location and upload photos for WexEnergy using Augmented Reality from a mobile device camera so that ordering and manufacturing can be done, accurately, quickly and efficiently. Furthermore, the app will be designed in a way for customers and distributors to easily keep track of various window measurements for each room in a building.
